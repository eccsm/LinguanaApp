# Privacy Policy for Linguana

**Effective Date:** December 2, 2025  
**Last Updated:** December 2, 2025

## Introduction

Linguana ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Audio Data (Microphone Permission)
- **Purpose:** Linguana uses your device's microphone for language learning features including:
  - Speech recognition for pronunciation practice
  - Voice-based exercises and interactions
  - Real-time audio feedback for language learning
- **Storage:** Audio recordings are processed locally on your device and are NOT permanently stored
- **Sharing:** Audio data is NOT shared with third parties
- **Control:** You can revoke microphone permission at any time through your device settings

### 2. Personal Information
When you create an account, we may collect:
- Email address
- Name (if provided)
- Authentication credentials (via Firebase Authentication)

### 3. Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- App usage statistics
- Device information (model, OS version)
- App performance data
- Crash reports

### 4. Storage Access
- **Purpose:** To save learning progress, downloaded content, and user preferences
- **Storage:** Data is stored locally on your device

### 5. Advertising ID
- **Purpose:** To serve personalized advertisements through Google AdMob
- **What is it:** A unique identifier for advertising purposes assigned by Google Play Services
- **How it's used:** To show relevant ads and measure ad performance
- **Control:** You can reset or opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads)
- **Sharing:** Shared only with Google AdMob for advertising purposes

## How We Use Your Information

- Improve app functionality and user experience

## Additional Information for Specific Permissions

### RECORD_AUDIO Permission
- **Why we need it:** To enable speech recognition and pronunciation practice features
- **When we use it:** Only when you actively use voice-based learning features
- **What we do:** Process audio locally for immediate feedback
- **What we DON'T do:** Store, upload, or share your voice recordings

### INTERNET Permission
- **Why we need it:** To sync learning progress, authenticate users, and load content
- **Data transmitted:** Encrypted authentication tokens, learning progress, app analytics

### ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Permission
- **Why we need it:** To check network connectivity before syncing data

### READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Permission
- **Why we need it:** To access downloaded learning materials and content
- **What we access:** Only app-specific files and user-selected content

### AD_ID Permission
- **Why we need it:** Required for apps using Google AdMob advertising on Android 13+
- **What it does:** Allows the app to access the Advertising ID for personalized ads
- **Your control:** You can reset your Advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ads in device settings
- **Industry standard:** This is a standard practice for all apps that show advertisements
